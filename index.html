THE INFORMATION BEHIND OUR SITE

Here, we are creating a website portal named District Portal of India.
Firstly the students studying in different institutes and other peoples who have read problem statement and are willing to submit their ideas about any problem will have to register themselves and create a login id & password to enter and submit their ideas through the portal then the people who are submitting their ideas can also submit any important problem which they faced in their district and after the verification of the authority and that problem will be uploaded in the portal will be easily sorted which will make at much easier to find desired problem statements. Here, each problem have a definite code about that specific problem and by entering the code which are seen in the portal. Different problems of different departments will be in the folders of the individual departments can be easily sorted by giving state and city of which they want to see and each problem which will be uploaded in this portal. When account is login by the user then it will be able to see the the problems according to their interest.Their will be like and comment buttons in each problems where users can give their feedback about that problem; so that common peoples hit like if it seem good and also they can give their feedback about that problem. The problem statements with most likes will be displayed on the home page which peoples can see before logging in. Also their wiil be a notification bar on the home page on which the people will get notifications. Here, also user can interact with the assigned authority on the personal chat when they are leaving their message related to their problems and they will get a "Talk to Authority" on each statement.
